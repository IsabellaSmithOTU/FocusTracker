Focus Tracker uses a deep learning model trained on the DAiSEE dataset (Dataset for Affective States in E-Environments), which contains thousands of videos of students engaged in online learning activities.

Our ResNet-18 based architecture analyzes your facial expressions, head pose, and eye movements across multiple frames to accurately predict your engagement level in real-time.

The Process:

  • Frame Capture: Every 8 seconds, we capture a snapshot from your webcam
  • Batch Processing: We analyze 5 consecutive frames together for better accuracy
  • Feature Extraction: ResNet-18 extracts 512 visual features from each frame
  • Temporal Pooling: Features are averaged across frames to capture temporal patterns
  • Classification: A final layer predicts your engagement level (0-3)
  • Consistency Check: Notifications only appear after 30 seconds of consistent engagement

Technical Details:

  • Model Architecture: ResNet-18 backbone + custom classifier
  • Input Size: 64√ó64 pixels (5 frames per prediction)
  • Processing: Local Python API (Flask) running on your machine
  • Normalization: ImageNet mean/std for optimal performance
  • Inference Time: ~200-300ms per prediction
